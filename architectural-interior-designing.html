3D Architectural Visualisation through 3DS MAX

Duration : 3 Months
3D Architectural Visualisation through 3DS MAX course designed for people who are interested to learn computer aided modeling using the industry proven softwares and to make a good understanding of how these softwares can help them to become better in the field of architecture and interior design.

Entry Criteria (For Students)

+  10+2 or Equivalent (Any Stream) with good English communication skills and creative bent of mind.
+  Admission is based on performance in the creative aptitude test and personal interview with the academic manager

Course Content

+  Fundamentals of 3ds max
+  Architectural design with 3dsmax
+  Structure Modeling
+  Engineering with 3dsmax
+  Photorealistic texturing
+  Environment design
+  Advanced lighting for realistic environment
+  Interior design and modeling
+  Interior shading and texturing
+  Beautification through interior lighting
+  Mentalray, Vray Texturing and rendering
+  Camera management
+  Walkthrough of a project
+  Pre-visualization
+  Rendering
+  Project

Opportunity

After completing this training a student will be able to use 3ds Max, VRay and Mentalray to make photorealistic architectural visualizations and you will be able to present your designs in a very creative way. With all this knowledge you can easily apply for any job in Interior Design and Architecture industry as
+  Interior designers
+  3d Architectural designers
