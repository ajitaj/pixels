Why Pixels Academy?

Imagine the opportunity to interact with the finest professionals in Hollywood, known to be the destination and hub of great animation, the likes of who have worked on diverse animation blockbusters including Rango, Transformers, Rise of the Guardians, Madagascar 3, The Legend of Secret Pass, to name a few.

Envisage the possibility of learning directly from them and getting your own work moulded to match standards set by world class studios like Disney, Pixar and Warner Bros.

That dream can be a reality now! Pixels Academy is here to translate such visions into reality. It is envisioned as a space where the limitlessness of the human imagination is brought to life with tools of creativity and technique through mentorship by the finest professionals across the VFX, Gaming and Animation industries. Pixels Academy believes in grooming its students to be industry-ready in every possible way; from technical know-how to presentation skills, from creative execution to learning how to tackle on-the-job challenges.

We strive to ensure that you learn and evolve across all dimensions. To put it in a nutshell, the calibre of training that you will receive; will enable you to work for internationally renowned companies, whose projects are creatively satisfying and widely accessible to the general population. So, what are you waiting for?

Vision & Mission

Vision
To provide the best world-class training to students in the field of Special Effects, Animation and Gaming, so that they are well-equipped and in sync with the latest knowledge and skill sets, to be readily absorbed into the industry.

Mission
*To cater to the needs of creatively inclined people who are inspired and motivated to create a sensation in the animation, VFX and gaming industry.
*To provide the best educational experience conducive to the holistic development of our students, both artistically and professionally.
*To bridge the gap and link the next generation animators with Hollywood's finest in the animation, gaming and VFX industry; providing them with an exposure to cutting-edge technology and unparalleled creativity.
